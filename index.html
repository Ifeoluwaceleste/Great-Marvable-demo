Who We Are

A Legacy of Learning & Character

Great Marvable Schools has been a pillar of quality education, combining rigorous academics with holistic development for both primary and secondary students.

Our Primary School

From nursery to Primary 6, we build strong literacy, numeracy, and curiosity in young learners through play-based and structured learning in a safe, nurturing environment.

Our Secondary School

Our JSS and SSS programmes prepare students for WAEC, NECO, and beyond — with strong science labs, arts studios, and dedicated coaching that has produced outstanding results year after year.

Our Community

We are more than a school — we are a family. Parents, teachers, and students collaborate in a vibrant community rooted in respect, excellence, and continuous growth.

Great Marvable Schools was founded on the belief that every child — regardless of background — deserves access to world-class education. Our curriculum is designed to challenge, inspire, and develop the whole child.

We are proudly accredited and our graduates go on to excel in top universities and careers both in Nigeria and internationally.
